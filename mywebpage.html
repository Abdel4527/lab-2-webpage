The Real Madrid club is a professional football team based in the Spanish capital of Barcelona and one of Europe's most successful teams

Real Madrid in 2017 was a football club that continued to solidify its status as one of the most successful and prestigious teams in the world. Led by manager Zinedine Zidane and featuring a star-studded roster, they achieved significant success during that year. Here are some of the key highlights and events for Real Madrid in 2017:

  1. UEFA Champions League Triumph: Real Madrid's crowning achievement in 2017 was winning the UEFA Champions League for the third consecutive year, becoming the first team to achieve such a feat in the modern era of the competition. They defeated Juventus 4-1 in the final, held in Cardiff, Wales. Cristiano Ronaldo was instrumental in this victory, scoring twice in the final.

  2. La Liga Title Real Madrid also clinched the La Liga title in 2016-2017 season. They finished the campaign with 93 points, beating their arch-rivals Barcelona by three points. It was their first La Liga title since 2012.

  3. Club World Cup: Real Madrid continued their success on the global stage by winning the FIFA Club World Cup in 2017. They defeated Brazilian side Grêmio 1-0 in the final to claim the trophy.

  4. Spanish Super Cup In August 2017, Real Madrid won the Spanish Super Cup, defeating Barcelona 5-1 on aggregate over two legs. This victory was particularly sweet for Real Madrid fans as it came at the expense of their fierce rivals.
